Product Description

S100 TG Micro case comes with one tempered glass panel on the left, one preinstalled 120mm standard fan at the rear and supports motherboards up to Micro-ATX. The S100 Tempered Glass Edition Micro Chassis combines sophistication, elegance, and simplicity and is built with modern steel construction. Based on the key features of S Series chassis TG, the S100 TG is designed to be even more compact and is manufactured with one tempered glass window on the left for a stunning view and one preinstalled 120mm standard fan at the rear for fundamental ventilation. The flexibility is limitless for high-end components. The S100 TG Micro Chassis is a great fit for users who are looking for a budget friendly and stylish chassis.

    FEATURES
  • Slim and Compact Design: Full metal construction around the body with sleek front panel design, the S100 TG Micro Chassis demonstrates a slim and compact design style.
  • Built-in PSU Cover:The built-in ventilated PSU cover design can improve airflow and hide those unsightly cables.
  • Tempered Glass Window: The 3mm tempered glass window on the side of the S100 TG is built to be more durable, scratch resistant, and transparent for an advanced view of your hardware.
  • Dust Reduction: A handy magnetic fan filter on the top simply attaches for easy dust filtration and particulate protection. At the base of the chassis, additional fan filters can provide excellent protection against both dirt and dust.
  • Hardware Expansion: S100 TG has excellent expandability. It can support a CPU cooler with a maximum height of 165mm, VGA up to 330mm long, and a full length power supply of up to 160mm. The case comes with a preinstalled 120 mm rear exhaust fan optimized for excellent cooling capabilities. S100 TG is able to house up to two 140mm or Three 120mm fans at the front and a radiator of up to 280mm at the front and on the top to allowing users to build their desired high-end system.
  • Handy I/O Ports: One 3.0 USB and two 2.0 USB ports on the top-front panel to grants direct access when needed.
